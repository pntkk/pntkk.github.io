Pineapple Cake

  • Time: 40 minutes
  • Serves: 5
Ingredients Quantity
Pineapple 2
Water 0.5 lit
Flour 1 kg
Eggs 5

Step 1: Seperate the yolk from the eggs.
Step 2: Mix the white part with water and flour.
Step 3: Put them into the oven and bake.

Pineapple Juice

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 5
Ingredients Quantity
Pineapple 4
Water 2 lits
Sugar 300 grams

Step 1: Peel the pineapples.
Step 2: Chop them to small pieces.
Step 3: Put water and pineapples into the blender and mix them together.
